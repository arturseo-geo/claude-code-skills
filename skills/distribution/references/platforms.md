# Platform Accounts & Rules

## Gumroad
- **Store URL**: thegeolab.gumroad.com
- **Custom domain**: store.thegeolab.net (verified but /l/slug 404s — Gumroad routing bug, contact support)
- **Working links**: thegeolab.gumroad.com/l/[slug]
- **Description format**: Plain text only (no HTML accepted)
- **MCP available**: Yes — rmarescu/gumroad-mcp (read products, offer codes, enable/disable — NO create/update product)
- **Gumroad Discover**: Requires at least 1 successful sale + Risk Review — won't work for free ($0+) products
- **Tags**: Add to all products for discoverability
- **Cover**: 816x1302px works, 1280x1920px recommended

## Payhip
- **Description**: Plain text, 50-4,000 characters
- **Rules**: No email addresses, hyperlinks, prices, or promotions in description
- **Category**: No dedicated "E-Books" category — check dropdown for closest match
- **Cover**: 2560x1600 LANDSCAPE (unique among all platforms)
- **Blog posts**: Payhip has a blog feature for content marketing
- **MCP**: None available

## Draft2Digital (D2D) / Writing Life
- **URL**: draft2digital.com
- **Daily limit**: 3 new titles per 24 hours — creating additional accounts to bypass = account termination
- **Description**: Plain text, 50-4,000 characters, no links/prices/promos
- **EPUBCheck**: Must pass EPUB 3.0 validation
- **Free ISBN**: Available (D2D-assigned)
- **Rights**: "I am the original creator of this content"
- **Print book**: Optional (D2D Print)
- **Cover minimum**: 1600x2400px
- **Categories**: BISAC codes (e.g., BUSINESS & ECONOMICS / Marketing / Search Engine Optimization)
- **Distributes to**: Apple Books, B&N, Kobo, Scribd, Tolino, Vivlio, OverDrive, Smashwords, cloudLibrary, Hoopla, Fable, Gardners, Bookshop.org, BorrowBox
- **No separate accounts needed**: D2D distributes to Kobo, Apple, B&N on your behalf
- **MCP**: None available

## Amazon KDP
- **URL**: kdp.amazon.com
- **Price minimum**: $0.99 (cannot list free directly)
- **Permafree**: List at $0.99, then report lower price from Gumroad/Payhip ($0) — Amazon price-matches to free
- **DRM**: Select "No" for maximum distribution
- **KDP Select**: Say "No" if publishing on other platforms (D2D, Kobo, Apple)
- **Accessibility**: "This book does not contain images necessary for understanding" for text-based guides
- **Review time**: Up to 72 hours after submission
- **No daily upload limit**
- **Cover minimum**: 1600x2560px (5:8 ratio)
- **Keywords**: 7 fields, 50 chars each, long-tail phrases only
- **Categories**: 2 during upload, up to 10 via Author Central support
- **MCP**: None available

## Google Play Books
- **URL**: play.google.com/books/publish
- **Description**: Plain text, up to 4,000 characters
- **Rules**: No links, no promotional language
- **Royalty**: 52% standard, 70% partner program (invite only)
- **Free**: Supported (list at $0 directly)
- **Review time**: Up to 48 hours
- **Cover minimum**: 1280x1920px (1600x2400 recommended)
- **Categories**: Google-specific taxonomy (similar to BISAC)
- **ISBN**: Optional
- **Payout**: Monthly, $1 minimum threshold
- **DRM**: Optional
- **MCP**: None available
