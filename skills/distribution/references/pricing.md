# Pricing Strategies

## Royalty Tiers by Platform

### Gumroad
- **Fee**: 10% flat fee on all sales
- **Royalty**: 90% to creator
- **Free**: $0+ (pay what you want) supported
- **Minimum paid**: $0.99
- **Payout**: Fridays (weekly), $10 minimum
- **Currency**: USD default, supports multi-currency

### Payhip
- **Fee**: 5% transaction fee (Free plan), 2% (Plus at $29/mo), 0% (Pro at $99/mo)
- **Royalty**: 95% on Free plan
- **Free**: Supported
- **Minimum paid**: $0.99
- **Payout**: Instant to PayPal or Stripe
- **Best for**: Highest royalty on direct sales

### Amazon KDP
- **35% royalty tier**: Any price ($0.99-$200), all markets, no delivery fee
- **70% royalty tier**: $2.99-$9.99 only, delivery fee deducted (~$0.06/MB)
- **Permafree**: Not directly available — see permafree strategy below
- **KDP Select (exclusive)**: Access to Kindle Unlimited (KU) pool, paid per page read (KENPC)
- **Payout**: 60 days after month-end, direct deposit
- **Currency**: Pays in local currency per marketplace

### Draft2Digital (D2D) / Writing Life
- **Fee**: ~10-15% of list price (varies by retailer)
- **Effective royalty**: ~55-65% depending on retailer
- **Free**: Supported on most retailers
- **Payout**: Monthly, 60 days after month-end, $10 minimum
- **D2D direct store**: 70% royalty (books sold on D2D's own store)

### Apple Books (via D2D)
- **Apple direct**: 70% royalty
- **Via D2D**: ~60% (Apple 70% minus D2D's cut)
- **Free**: Supported
- **Payout**: Via D2D monthly schedule

### Kobo (via D2D)
- **Kobo direct**: 70% ($2.99+), 45% (under $2.99)
- **Via D2D**: ~60% (Kobo's rate minus D2D's cut)
- **Kobo Plus**: Subscription pool (similar to KU), available via D2D
- **Free**: Supported

### Google Play Books
- **Royalty**: 52% (standard), 70% (partner program, invite only)
- **Free**: Supported
- **Payout**: Monthly, $1 minimum threshold

## Pricing Strategies

### Permafree Strategy (for lead magnets / first-in-series)
1. Set price to $0 on Gumroad and Payhip (instant)
2. Set price to $0 on D2D — distributes free to Apple, Kobo, B&N
3. List on KDP at $0.99 (minimum)
4. Once free on other platforms, go to KDP > Contact Us > report lower price
5. Amazon price-matches to $0 within 1-7 days (not guaranteed)
6. Monitor — Amazon may revert price; re-report if needed

### $0.99 Entry Strategy
- Use $0.99 for short guides, lead magnets, or launch promotions
- KDP: Only 35% royalty at this price ($0.35 per sale)
- Gumroad: 90% royalty ($0.89 per sale)
- Best used temporarily for ranking/reviews, then raise to $2.99+

### Sweet Spot Pricing ($2.99-$9.99)
- KDP 70% royalty tier kicks in at $2.99
- Most indie ebook sales cluster at $2.99-$4.99
- $4.99: Good balance of perceived value and volume
- $9.99: Upper end of 70% tier — works for comprehensive guides

### Bundle / Library Pricing
- Price bundle at 40-60% of individual total
- Example: 9 books at $4.99 each = $44.91 → bundle at $19.99-$24.99
- Gumroad supports bundle products natively
- KDP does not support bundles — publish as single combined EPUB

### KDP Select vs. Wide Distribution

| Factor | KDP Select (Exclusive) | Wide Distribution |
|---|---|---|
| Kindle Unlimited | Yes (page reads revenue) | No |
| Other platforms | Not allowed | Gumroad, Payhip, Apple, Kobo, B&N |
| Royalty on sales | 70% (KDP) | Varies (70-95% depending on platform) |
| Enrollment | 90-day auto-renewing | No commitment |
| Best for | Fiction, KU-heavy genres | Non-fiction, building direct audience |
| Permafree | Not available in Select | Available via price-match |

**Recommendation for non-fiction/guides**: Go wide. Non-fiction readers buy rather than borrow, so KU page reads are lower. Direct platforms (Gumroad, Payhip) offer better royalties and customer data.

## Price Parity Rules

- **Amazon**: Will price-match lower prices found elsewhere (can work for or against you)
- **D2D retailers**: Expect price parity — don't price lower on your own store than on theirs
- **Gumroad/Payhip**: You control pricing independently, but if Amazon detects a lower price, they may match it without asking
- **Strategy**: Keep prices equal across all platforms, or use Gumroad/Payhip as "premium" (includes PDF + EPUB) at same or slightly higher price

## Offer Codes & Discounts

| Platform | Discount Support | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Gumroad | Offer codes (via MCP or dashboard) | Percentage or fixed amount, limited or unlimited uses |
| Payhip | Coupon codes | Percentage off, expiration dates |
| KDP | Countdown Deals (Select only) | Time-limited price drops, 1 per 90-day enrollment |
| D2D | No direct discount mechanism | Price changes only |
