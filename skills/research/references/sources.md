## Research Methodology Validation

**Before citing a source:**
1. Check publication date (freshness)
2. Verify author credentials
3. Look for funding bias
4. Cross-reference 2+ sources
5. Note confidence level

**Red flags:**
- ❌ Source is 3+ years old (use as historical context only)
- ❌ Author has obvious conflict of interest
- ❌ No sources cited in research
- ❌ Extraordinary claims without evidence
- ❌ Outlier data point unchallenged by author
